Crystal Collector!

Objective:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

You have 4 crystals to chose from. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game if your total score is greater than the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden and change at the start of each new game.
